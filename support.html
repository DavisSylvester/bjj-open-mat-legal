Frequently asked

  • How do I find open mats near me? Allow location access, then use the Discover / Search screen to see open mats by distance, day, and gi / no-gi.
  • How do I sign in? Use Sign in with Apple, Google, or email. No password is required for the social options.
  • How do I add my gym's open mat? Sign in, then submit a session from the app. Gym owners can manage their listings.
  • How do I check in? Open the session when you arrive at the gym and tap check in — location helps confirm you're there.
  • How do I delete my account? Email us at the address above and we'll remove your account and personal data.
